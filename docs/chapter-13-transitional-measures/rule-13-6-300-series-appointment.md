# Rule 13.6: 300-series appointment

> Source: `un2018.pdf`, p. 89

A staff member holding a 300-series short-term appointment as at 30 June 2009
shall continue to serve the period of the appointment until the expiration date
specified in his or her letter of appointment. Eff ective 1 July 2009, 300-series short term appointments shall be governed by the terms and conditions applicable to
temporary appointments under the Staff Regulations and the Staff Rules.
