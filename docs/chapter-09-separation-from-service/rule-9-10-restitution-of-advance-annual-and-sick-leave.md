# Rule 9.10: Restitution of advance annual and sick leave

> Source: `un2018.pdf`, p. 75

On separation, a staff member who has taken advance annual or sick le ave
beyond that which he or she has subsequently accrued shall make restitution for such
advance leave by means of a cash refund or an offset against monies due to the staff
member from the United Nations, equivalent to the remuneration received, including
allowances and other payments, in respect of the advance leave period. The Secretary General may waive this requirement if in his or her opinion there are exceptional or
compelling reasons for doing so.
